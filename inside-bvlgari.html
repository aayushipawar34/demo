Our Legacy

Bvlgari was founded in Rome in 1884 by the talented Greek silversmith Sotirio Bulgari. The brand quickly established a reputation for Italian excellence, exquisite craftsmanship, and magnificent jewellery creations that blend classicism with contemporary boldness.

Our Values

At the core of Bvlgari is a passion for beauty, heritage, creativity, and sustainability. We believe in crafting timeless treasures while preserving the environment, empowering artisans, and uplifting communities.

Sustainability & Craftsmanship

From sourcing responsibly to supporting global education initiatives, Bvlgari is deeply committed to building a better future through its craftsmanship, community outreach, and green innovations.
